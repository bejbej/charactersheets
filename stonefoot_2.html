Deus Ex Animalis

Stonefoot is generally always hiding nearby, but will not willingly enter combat. If Barick ever drops to 0 health, Stonefoot will sprint into combat to protect him.

Keen Hearing and Smell

Stonefoot has advantage on Perception checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Pack Tactics

Stonefoot has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

Favored Enemy: Beast

Stonefoot gains a +2 bonus to damage rolls with weapon attacks against beasts. Additionally, Stonefoot has advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks to track beasts, as well as on Intelligence checks to recall information about them.
